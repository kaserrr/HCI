Grim Prison Cell
A game ready low poly interior ready to be used in games. 
This room is intended to be low-poly with a dirty asthetic and low resolution
textures.

!!!This room is optimized for Forward+ and Compatibility in Godot Game Engine!!!

Technical Specs:
	Total Polygons: 4,216 Triangles
	Total Vertices: 2,956 Vertices
	Total Objects: 37
	Tested In: Godot 4.6.2.stable
	Scale: 1 unit = 1 meter
	
Folder Structure:
	res://
	ReadyScenes
		prison_cell.tscn (Import this into your game)
	Scenes
		> All the RAW data if you want to tweak anything.
	Textures
		> Contains all the textures.
	readme.txt

Textures look better with the Specular turned to 0 to give it a more retro look.
Shadows look great when an Omni-Light is placed right at the "Lightbulb"'s location.

	
Room dimensions:
	3 x 5 room with door in the center front wall.
	Door can be slid for a prison vibe.
	No windows.
	
Included:
	1x Prison cell like room with a food slot. The hatch
	can be rotated manually and conforms to traditional rotations as 
	if it has a hinge. 
	
	1x Slab bed with a terribly uncomfortable, scratchy pillow and worn out
	mattress.
	
	1x Sanitation combo complete with hot water though the quality and cleanliness
	of the water and sanitation area are debatable.
	
	1x mountain of cans to emphasise the "lived-in" state of the room. "Yummy-Yum, Meat in a Can" cans
	are everywhere in the room to further emphasise this idea.
	
	1x Loudspeaker for additional options.
	
	A weathered floor and a set of walls with one singular light ready to be turned into a
	light. The light bulb is tweakable with an emission texture already applied. 
	
	All objects have collision except for a few small things, but nothing should get in the player's way.
	
	All materials are inculded in the "Textures" folder and kept with the same naming convention from ambientCG
	All materials used in this asset pack are licensed under Creative Commons Zero (CC0) and align with all 
	international copyright standards and legal requirements for redistribution.
